# Welcome
Welcome to MAFT Foundation. We are Foundation from Spain established in 2025 by the founders of Balneario de Cofrentes (balneario.com) 
We want to help humanity reach 120 years of age with good health. 
We do this by supporting technology and biotech companies through grants and early investments.
You can learn more about us on maftfoundation.com
